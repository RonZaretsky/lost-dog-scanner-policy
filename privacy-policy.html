Privacy Policy for Lost Dog Scanner

Last updated: March 17, 2025

Lost Dog Scanner respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It only retrieves public Facebook group posts using the Facebook Graph API.

Data Usage

This app uses the Facebook Graph API to fetch posts. No other third-party services are involved.
